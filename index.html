Export Process

We align growers/farmers, pack houses, and logistics partners under strict QA to deliver consistent, compliant shipments—every time

01 Sourcing & QC

Lot selection, grading, moisture/size tests

We contract with certified farms and run pre-harvest checks. Brix, moisture and size grading ensure uniformity. On‑site inspectors approve lots before harvest.

02 Packing & Labeling

Retail/wholesale packs, private labels

Produce is hydro‑cooled where needed and packed in food‑grade corrugates/crates. Barcodes, MRP (if required), and destination‑specific labels are applied.

03 Compliance Docs

Phytosanitary, CO, invoice, packing list

We arrange full documentation per destination norms: Phytosanitary Certificate, Certificate of Origin, health certs, fumigation (if needed) and export invoice/PL.

04 Logistics

Cold‑chain, port handling, sailing/airlift

Temperature‑controlled (reefer) trucks move goods to port. We handle customs, stuffing and booking on the fastest routes via sea or air as per shelf‑life.

  • PO & 30% advance
  • Harvest & packing (2–4 days)
  • Documentation & customs (1–2 days)
  • Dispatch & tracking shared
